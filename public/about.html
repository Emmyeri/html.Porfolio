Meet Emmanuel Chukwuemeka-Eri

My journey

My educational journey began at Central School in Ifite Aguleri, Nigeria, where I completed my primary education. I then attended Archbishop Patterson Seminary and later St. Christopher’s Junior Seminary in Onitsha, Nigeria, where I earned my Senior School Certificate. My keen interest in languages led me to Pinnacle Center for German Studies in Awka, Nigeria, where I completed a comprehensive German language course from A1 to C1 levels.

Currently, I am advancing my academic career at the University of Hamburg, preparing myself for the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.

professional Experience

I have been fortunate to gain diverse professional experiences. As a German language teacher at St. Patrick’s College in Awka, Nigeria, I had the privilege of educating students in the basics of the German language. This role honed my teaching skills and deepened my appreciation for education. My work as a First Aid Intern at St. Christopher’s Junior Seminary Social Club allowed me to contribute to my community by providing essential first aid services and training. Additionally, my tenure as a Sales Associate at Vido Exchange in Prague, Czech Republic, equipped me with valuable sales and customer service skills.

Skills and Interests

My technical skills include proficiency in Microsoft Office and HTML, along with foundational knowledge of C# and CSS. I am fluent in English, my native language, and possess proficient German language skills, certified at the Goethe C1 level.

Beyond my professional and academic pursuits, I have a passion for playing football and chess, activities that keep me engaged and mentally sharp.
